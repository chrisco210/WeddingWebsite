Accommodations

Hotels

Unfortunately it is Cornell move in weekend so we recommend not staying in Ithaca. Horseheads/Corning and Watkins glen will be much less chaotic. Some hotels we recommend are located near the Elmira Corning Regional Airport: We will arrange transportation to these hotels since they are located close to each other, so any other hotel near the ones listed above will work as well.

  • Courtyard Marriott Elmira Horseheads
  • Candlewood Suites Horseheads - Elmira by IHG
  • Country Inn & Suites by Radisson, Big Flats (Elmira), NY

AirBNBs

There are several AirBNBs located very close on Cayuta Lake. Elena's mom has reserved a few, so please reach out to her if you are interested in staying in one of them. If you are doing your own search, try to find a rental closer to Watkins Glen (make sure it is located on the south side of Seneca Lake), or in Elmira or Corning, NY. Let us know if you have any questions!

Campground

There are several campgrounds nearby: let Allison or Tik know if you would like to use their camper!

  • The Cool Lea Campground on Cayuta Lake
  • Tent sites at Taughannock Falls State Park
