Venue Details

The wedding ceremony will be held on August 15, 2026 at 4:00 pm in the afternoon on the grounds of the Fontainebleau Inn. The Inn is located at 2800 NY-228, in Alpine, New York, which is about ~30 minutes from Ithaca, ~20 minutes from Watkins Glen, and ~30 minutes from the Corning/Horseheads area. Following the ceremony, we are delighted to invite you to the reception, which will be held at the Inn.

Note: the inn is on Cayuta Lake (not Cayuga lake).

After the ceremony, please proceed up the Hill to the Inn for a cocktail hour with music provided by Mike Titlebaum (on sax), Catherine Gale (vocals) and friends. Formal attire is requested.

Parking and Transportation

We have hired a van driver to transport anyone home when they need. We would encourage anyone enjoying the celebration with alcohol not to drive. Please don't drink and drive on these dark, country roads.

Welcome Party at Taughannock Falls State Park

The Chatrchyan's and Rachlinski's are hosting a Welcome Party for our out-of-town guests. Join us for an Armenian Barbecue at the Taughannock Falls State Park Central Shelter, from 6-8pm on Friday, August 14th. We will have Armenian khorovats with grilled pork, vegetables and lavash; pulled chicken; and beverages.

Bring your swimsuit and towel as swimming in Cayuga Lake is wonderful. There is also a children's playground at the Park.

Brunch: Sunday, August 16th

For our out-of-town guests, please stop by the Chatrchyan's house for a brunch with Allison & Tigran, and Jeff and Melissa from 10:30-12:30 before you hit the road. We will have bagels, lox and charcuterie, Grandma Gert's coffee cake, fruit and lots of coffee! We are located at 505 Lake Road, Dryden, NY 13053.
