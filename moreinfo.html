Activities

We’re so excited for you to join us in the Finger Lakes. If you’re spending extra time in the area, here are some of our favorite places to explore around Alpine, NY (near Ithaca and Watkins Glen).

Wineries & Tasting Rooms

  • Hermann J. Wiemer Vineyard — A Finger Lakes classic, known for world-class Rieslings.
  • Standing Stone Vineyards — Gorgeous views over Seneca Lake and a relaxed tasting atmosphere.
  • Sheldrake Point Winery — A lakeside gem on Cayuga Lake, perfect for sipping wine outdoors.
  • Red Newt Cellars — Great wines and a cozy tasting room that makes you want to linger.

Parks & Outdoors

  • Buttermilk Falls State Park — Our favorite spot for hiking and waterfall views; trails for all levels and picnic areas.
  • Taughannock Falls State Park — Home to one of the tallest waterfalls east of the Rockies.
  • Watkins Glen State Park — Famous for its dramatic gorge trail and multiple waterfalls.

Favorite Restaurants

  • Le Cafe Cent Dix - French bistro, 3/4 beans
  • Gola Osteria - Italian, 3/4 beans

See more recommendations on our blog!

Nearby Towns

  • Ithaca — A vibrant college town with great restaurants, coffee shops, and the Ithaca Farmers Market.
  • Watkins Glen — Charming downtown with breweries, shops, and attractions like the race track.
